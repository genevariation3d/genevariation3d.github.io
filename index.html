Genevariation - 3D

A workshop to discuss interconnection of genetic variation data, PDB, Kaviar, PeptideAtlas, and TPP

A two-day workshop at the Institute for Systems Biology in Seattle, Washington, for collaborators and other researchers working toward the interconnection of genomic, proteomic, and protein structure data. Our ultimate goal is to tie together genetic variation data with protein level information, by using the four established resources as a driving force:

  • The RCSB Protein Data Bank
  • The Kaviar database of human genomic variation
  • The PeptideAtlas proteomics data compendium
  • The Trans-Proteomic Pipeline software

This workshop is funded by a Big Data Spoke Planning Grant and is part of the West Big Data Innovation Hub.

The workshop will take place sometime during the first two weeks of January, 2017.

Contact Eric Deutsch, edeutsch@systemsbiology.org, if you are interested in being invited.
